Seal Patrol

After losing contact with a clandestine energy research facility, a powerful venture capitalist contracts an elite team of operatives to retrieve the physicist who holds the key to an unprecedented alternate energy source. Upon arrival, the operatives are ambushed by an extra-dimensional creature that has mutilated the onsite personnel. The team must develop a new brand of warfare if there is to be any hope of making it out alive.

Nicolas Aaron Mezzanatto

Director

Army Veteran Nicolas Aaron Mezzanatto deployed with the 1-502nd Infantry 101st Airborne to the “Triangle of Death” southern Baghdad in 2005. The book “Blackhearts” by Jim Frederick was written about the units harrowing tour in Iraq.

Joshua Ringle

Producer

While enlisted in the United States Air Force, Joshua Ringle Deployed with the 332nd Expiditionary Security Forces Squaron to Balad, Iraq (Camp Anaconda) in 07-08 to assist with Joint Service Operations.
